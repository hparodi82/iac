## 🔥 Our Core Services

1. 🧯 **Fire Extinguisher Sales & Installation**  
   Class A, B, C & K extinguishers — mounted, tagged, and ready for use. Includes cabinets and initial certifications.

2. 🧾 **Annual Inspection & Recharging**  
   Licensed technicians provide inspection, hydrotesting, and refilling services per **NFPA 10** and state fire codes.

3. 🍳 **Kitchen Fire Suppression Systems**  
   Design, installation, and semi-annual inspection of Ansul, Kidde, and Pyro-Chem suppression systems.

4. 💡 **Emergency Lighting & Exit Signs**  
   Installation and certification testing to ensure your emergency systems function during power loss.

5. 🧹 **Hood & Duct Cleaning**  
   NFPA 96-compliant grease removal for restaurants and food-service operations.
