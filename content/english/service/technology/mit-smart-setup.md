### Technology That Works the Way You Do

A smart business or home office isn't just about convenience — it's about efficiency, security, and creating an environment that supports how you work. At **Up To Par Technologies**, we design and deploy integrated smart technology setups that connect your devices, automate routine tasks, and give you centralized control over your space.

---

### Business Smart Office Solutions

**Automated Lighting & Climate Control**
Occupancy-based lighting, scheduled HVAC control, and smart thermostats reduce energy costs and create a comfortable working environment without manual management.

**Conference Room & AV Setup**
We configure professional audio/visual systems for conference rooms and meeting spaces — display mounting, cable management, video conferencing integration (Microsoft Teams, Zoom), and wireless presentation systems.

**Smart Access Control**
Keycard, fob, or mobile-based door access systems that log entry and exit, restrict access by role or time, and integrate with your surveillance cameras for a complete security picture.

**Automated Door & Intercom Systems**
Video doorbells, intercom systems, and motorized entry points configured for professional use — with remote monitoring and access from any device.

**Centralized Device Management**
All smart devices — from access control to cameras to lighting — managed from a single dashboard, reducing complexity and giving your team or facilities manager full visibility.

### Home Office Solutions

For professionals working from home, we ensure your workspace is as capable as any corporate office:

- Dedicated high-performance Wi-Fi for your workspace (separate from home devices)
- Smart desk and lighting setup for video calls and focused work
- Secure VPN integration connecting your home office to the company network
- Printer, scanner, and peripheral configuration
- UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) setup to protect your equipment and keep you working through brief outages
- Backup internet (LTE failover) for critical remote workers
