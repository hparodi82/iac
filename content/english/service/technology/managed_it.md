### What's Included in Our Managed IT Plans

**24/7 Network & Systems Monitoring**
We monitor your servers, workstations, firewalls, and network infrastructure around the clock. Most issues are identified and resolved before your team even notices them.

**Helpdesk & End-User Support**
Your staff gets fast, knowledgeable support for day-to-day IT issues — password resets, software problems, hardware failures, and more. We keep your team productive so downtime stays minimal.

**Patch Management & Software Updates**
We keep your operating systems, applications, and firmware up to date with scheduled patching — closing security vulnerabilities before they can be exploited.

**Cybersecurity & Threat Monitoring**
From firewall management and antivirus oversight to endpoint detection and email security, we layer your defenses to protect your business from today's evolving threats.

**Backup & Disaster Recovery**
Your data is your most valuable asset. We implement automated, tested backup solutions and disaster recovery plans so you can recover quickly from any incident — hardware failure, ransomware, or accidental deletion.

**Vendor Management**
We coordinate with your internet providers, software vendors, and hardware suppliers on your behalf — so you have one point of contact instead of ten.

**IT Strategy & vCIO Services**
Beyond day-to-day support, we help you plan ahead. Our technology roadmap and virtual CIO (vCIO) services align your IT investments with your business goals, helping you budget smarter and avoid costly surprises.
