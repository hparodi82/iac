### See Everything. Miss Nothing.

A modern IP camera system does more than record video — it gives you real-time visibility into your facility, deters theft and unauthorized access, and provides the documented evidence you need when incidents occur. At **Up To Par Technologies**, we design and install professional-grade IP surveillance systems tailored to your space and security requirements.

---

### Our IP Camera Services

**System Design & Consultation**
We assess your facility's layout, identify coverage blind spots, and design a camera placement plan that maximizes visibility while minimizing equipment costs.

**Professional Installation**
Our technicians handle all wiring, mounting, and configuration — including PoE (Power over Ethernet) cabling runs, NVR/DVR setup, and network integration.

**Remote Viewing & Mobile Access**
View live and recorded footage from any device — smartphone, tablet, or desktop. We configure secure remote access so you always know what's happening at your location.

**Integration with Access Control**
We integrate surveillance systems with door access control and alarm systems for a unified security solution.

**Ongoing Monitoring & Support**
As part of your Managed IT plan, we monitor camera health, storage capacity, and system uptime — so you're never surprised by a camera that stopped recording.
