### Because "I Thought We Had a Backup" Is Not a Recovery Plan

Data loss happens. Ransomware, hardware failure, accidental deletion, natural disasters — the threats are real and they don't give advance notice. The only thing standing between a bad day and a business-ending event is a backup strategy that actually works. At **Up To Par Technologies**, we implement and manage comprehensive backup and disaster recovery solutions that are automated, monitored, and — most importantly — tested.

---

### Our Backup & Recovery Services

**Backup Strategy Design**
We evaluate what data you have, where it lives, how critical it is, and how quickly you need it back if something goes wrong. From that assessment, we design a tiered backup strategy aligned with your recovery time objectives (RTO) and recovery point objectives (RPO).

**Automated Local Backups**
We configure automated backups of your servers, workstations, and critical systems to local storage or NAS devices — providing fast, on-site recovery for everyday incidents.

**Offsite & Cloud Backups**
Local backups alone are not enough. We replicate your backups to a secure offsite location or cloud platform (Azure Backup, Veeam Cloud, Datto, or similar) — protecting you from site-level disasters like fire, flood, or theft.

**Microsoft 365 & Cloud Data Backup**
Microsoft does not back up your email, SharePoint, or OneDrive data on your behalf. We implement dedicated Microsoft 365 backup solutions to protect your cloud data from accidental deletion and ransomware encryption.

**Backup Monitoring & Alerting**
Every backup job is monitored. If a backup fails, we know about it immediately and resolve it — you don't find out your backups weren't working when you need them most.

**Disaster Recovery Planning**
We document a step-by-step recovery playbook for your environment — so that when an incident occurs, the response is fast, coordinated, and effective. We test recovery scenarios regularly, not just when a disaster forces the issue.

---

### Ransomware Protection

Modern ransomware specifically targets and encrypts backup files. Our backup strategies include immutable backups — copies that cannot be altered or deleted by ransomware — so you always have a clean restore point.

### Recovery Time That Matters

The cost of downtime grows by the hour. Our solutions are designed with fast recovery in mind — whether you need to restore a single deleted file in minutes or rebuild an entire server environment in hours.
