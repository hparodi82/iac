### Replace Your Old Phone System with Something That Works

Traditional landlines are expensive, inflexible, and loaded with features nobody uses. VoIP (Voice over IP) phone systems deliver better call quality, more functionality, and significantly lower monthly costs — all running over your existing internet connection. At **Up To Par Technologies**, we handle the entire transition from your old system to a modern VoIP solution without disrupting your operations.

---

### What We Provide

**System Design & Number Porting**
We design your phone system around your team structure — departments, extensions, call queues, and auto-attendants. We port your existing phone numbers so your clients always reach you on the same number.

**Hardware & Softphone Setup**
Whether your team prefers physical desk phones, headsets, or softphone apps on their computer or mobile device, we configure everything and make sure it works from day one.

**Auto-Attendant & Call Routing**
Professional greetings, business hours routing, voicemail-to-email, and call forwarding — configured to match how your business actually operates.

**Integration with Microsoft Teams & Business Tools**
Many of our VoIP solutions integrate directly with Microsoft Teams, CRM platforms, and other business applications for a seamless communication experience.

**Ongoing Management & Support**
Adding a new employee? Changing your hours? Moving to a new office? We handle all system changes quickly, and monitor call quality and uptime as part of your Managed IT plan.
