### Reliable Networks Built for How You Work Today

Whether your team works on-site, remotely, or across multiple locations, your network is the foundation everything runs on. At **Up To Par Technologies**, we design, implement, and manage both traditional on-premise networks and modern cloud-based infrastructure — so your business stays fast, secure, and connected no matter where work happens.

---

### Traditional Networking

We design and deploy structured, high-performance local area networks (LAN) and wide area networks (WAN) built around your facility's needs.

**What We Handle:**
- Network design and architecture planning
- Firewall deployment and configuration (Cisco, Palo Alto, Fortinet, SonicWall)
- Managed switches and VLAN segmentation
- Wireless access point installation and coverage planning
- VPN configuration for remote and hybrid teams
- Network rack installation and cable management
- Ongoing monitoring, maintenance, and support

---

### Cloud Networking

Cloud networking gives your business greater flexibility, scalability, and resilience — without the overhead of managing physical hardware at every location.

**Our Cloud Networking Services Include:**
- SD-WAN design and deployment
- Cloud-based firewall and security (Meraki, Fortinet, Zscaler)
- Microsoft Azure and AWS network integration
- Site-to-site and remote-access VPN solutions
- Multi-location connectivity and unified management
